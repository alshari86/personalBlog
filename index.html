QE is very important role during Software Development Cycle. It's not just testing work, it involes requirement review and feedback, the whole product knowledge crossing different domains.

Where do we involve QE

Once PM initiates a project and had a draft of the requirement. QE should be involved in requirement and design review.

Cause QE knows the product very well, QE will have a lot of sense on cases around each requirement especially some corner cases not known by other team members.

How does QE scope project effort

QE needs to scope testing effort from many different areas, including Test plan/strategy, test case design, case execution, automation, regression as well as LnP, accessibility, etc

How to do QE work successfully

Know product, design test plan and cases well, automation and regression, look at header for each part specifically
